# HR Workforce Analytics & Employee Attrition Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze workforce composition, employee attrition, retention patterns, and employee performance.

The project transforms HR employee data into interactive business insights that can support HR teams and management in understanding workforce trends and identifying potential areas for improvement.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard consists of three analytical pages:

### 1. Workforce Overview

Provides a high-level overview of the organization's workforce.

Key areas analyzed:

- Total and active employees
- Workforce distribution across departments
- Gender distribution
- Employee hiring trends
- Active vs terminated employees by department
- Manager satisfaction and engagement
- Average salary by department

### 2. Employee Attrition & Retention

Analyzes employee terminations and identifies potential attrition patterns.

Key areas analyzed:

- Total terminated employees
- Termination rate
- Voluntary vs involuntary termination
- Termination rate by department
- Employee terminations over time
- Top termination reasons
- Termination reasons by department
- Satisfaction, engagement, and absences by employment status

### 3. Employee Performance & Engagement

Examines employee performance and explores relationships between performance and employee experience.

Key areas analyzed:

- Performance distribution among active employees
- Performance across departments
- Performance by employment status
- Employee engagement and performance
- Employee satisfaction and performance
- Employee absences and performance
- High-performing employee percentage

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

This dashboard was designed to answer key HR and workforce management questions:

- What does the current workforce look like?
- How are employees distributed across departments?
- Which departments experience the highest termination rates?
- How has employee termination changed over time?
- Why are employees leaving the organization?
- Are termination reasons different across departments?
- How do voluntary and involuntary terminations differ?
- Are terminated employees different from active employees in terms of satisfaction and engagement?
- How does employee performance vary across departments?
- Is employee performance associated with engagement, satisfaction, or absenteeism?
